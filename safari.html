Safari

Explore the wilderness and witness wildlife in their natural habitat!

About Safari

Safari is an adventurous journey into the wild, where you can observe wildlife and nature closely. Modern safaris focus on photography and exploration rather than hunting.

Temporary camps are set up for lodging and fooding during multi-day safaris. It offers a unique experience of living close to nature.

Popular Safari Destinations in India

  • Kanha – Tigers, swamp deer, sloth bears, blackbucks, owls, hyenas, pythons
  • Bandhavgarh – Tigers, leopards, hyenas, deer, nilgais, birds
  • Ranthambore – Tigers, leopards, hyenas, deer, birds
  • Pench – Deer, foxes, hyenas, jungle fowl, peafowl
  • Panna – Wolves, tigers, hyenas, geese, vultures
  • Corbett – Bears, tigers, elephants, buffaloes, monkeys
  • Sunderbans – Dolphins, porpoises, storks, kingfishers
  • Kaziranga – One-horned rhinos, leopards, bears, jackals, vultures
  • Tadoba – Deer, tigers, cobras, eagles, spiders
  • Bandipur – Chitals, gaurs, dholes, langurs, snakes
  • Periyar – Bats, squirrels, macaques, kites, eagles, coral snakes

Safari provides an incredible opportunity to connect with nature, observe animals in their natural environment, and enjoy a peaceful yet thrilling outdoor experience.
