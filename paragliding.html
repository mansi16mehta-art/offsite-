About Paragliding

Paragliding is one of the most popular adventure sports in India. A jump from a slanted mountain edge gives you the sensation of flying over scenic landscapes. The thrill is unmatched!

Beginners and first-timers are guided by trained and certified pilots. Safety measures are strictly followed, and flights are weather dependent.

The pilot controls the glider’s direction, speed, and descent. Some flights include acrobatics for enthusiasts. Popular locations include Manali and Bhimtal (2000–2500 ft), with higher flights at around 5000 ft.

Safety Guidelines

  • Fly only with certified pilots
  • Check weather conditions before flight
  • Follow all instructions carefully
  • Children above 8 years and adults in good health can participate

Paragliding offers breathtaking views of valleys, mountains, and rivers, and is an unforgettable adventure activity.
