About Rafting

Rafting is a high-adrenaline water sport where you navigate rivers in inflatable rafts. It offers excitement, teamwork, and a chance to challenge yourself.

Depending on river conditions, rafting can range from calm flows to rapid, choppy waters. Each rafting session is led by an experienced guide who ensures safety and navigation.

Participants wear life jackets and helmets for protection. Even non-swimmers can enjoy rafting safely under proper guidance.

Popular Rafting Locations in India

  • Rishikesh – Ganga River
  • Manali – Beas River
  • Kolad – Kundalika River
  • Garhwal – Tons River

Rapids are graded according to difficulty. Experienced guides provide briefing and instructions for safety. Rafting combines adventure, teamwork, and the beauty of nature in one thrilling experience.
