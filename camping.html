About Camping

Camping is a recreational activity connecting oneself with nature and basics, pursued outdoors away from cities. Experience fresh air, birds chirping, flowing rivers, and open skies. Evening campfires under a starry sky, hills offering breathtaking views, streams nearby, and open Machans for sleeping under stars.

Camping is often combined with other adventure activities like rock climbing, rappelling, nature walks, river crossing, bird watching, village tours, hiking, rafting, kayaking, and inhouse sports.

India offers camping in mountains, deserts, and wildlife parks. Destinations like Nainital, Rishikesh, Mashobra, Ranthambore, Corbett, Shimla, Manali, and Leh provide different camping experiences.

Desert camps in Pushkar and Jaisalmer provide golden sand dunes, camel rides, and evening Banjara Dance. Wilderness camps like Corbett and Ranthambore offer a jungle experience.
