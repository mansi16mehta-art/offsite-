About Rock Climbing

Rock climbing is a physically and mentally challenging sport that involves climbing up natural rock formations using your hands and feet. It tests endurance, strength, balance, and focus.

Climbing is performed using safety harnesses, ropes, and other equipment. Routes vary in difficulty from beginner-friendly to expert-level challenges.

Rock climbing can be a team-building activity, encouraging cooperation and trust among participants.
