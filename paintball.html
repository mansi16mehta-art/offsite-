Paintball

Teamwork, strategy, and adrenaline – experience real action!

About Paintball

Paintball is a fast-growing adventure sport, played in over 200 countries. It combines speed, strategy, and teamwork. The goal is to tag opponents with paintballs while avoiding being hit. Paintball is highly action-packed and adrenaline-pumping.

Players use semi-automatic markers (guns), wear facemasks, chest guards, and jumpsuits. Bright colored paintballs are biodegradable and safe.

Corporate Paintball

Paintball is an ideal corporate training tool, developing mental strategies, teamwork, and physical activity. It is considered the best management game in the world.

Rules & Equipment

  • Markers (guns) powered by CO2 tanks
  • Protective facemasks and chest guards
  • Team-based strategic games

Paintball is not just shooting – it involves thinking, running, aiming, and working with your team to achieve objectives. Safety gear is mandatory.
