About Hot Air Ballooning

Along with friends and loved ones, slowly ascend in the air and enjoy the thrill of floating above landscapes. Hot air ballooning in India offers breathtaking views and an unforgettable experience.

The balloon is inflated with hot air via a burner, making it lighter than air and allowing it to rise. Pilots control the altitude by adjusting the temperature inside the balloon. Wind direction guides horizontal movement.

Hot air balloon flights generally last around 3 hours and can reach elevations of approximately 1200 feet. Age should be above 8 years and height above 4 feet. People with heart ailments, high blood pressure, epilepsy, psychiatric conditions, or pregnancy should avoid this activity.

Locations in India

  • Jaipur
  • Lonavala
  • Goa
  • Ranthambore

Hot air ballooning includes modern safety measures, skilled pilots, and necessary instruments for navigation.
